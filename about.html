About The Developers

FAQ

1. What is our goal?

Our team aims to make Makinabang a risk-free zone. The primary goal of our website is to assist residents and raise awareness of the hazard through a social platform. We came up with this idea to reduce the possibility of hazardous areas in Makinabang. This will also raise people's social awareness.

2. What can I expect to see?

you can find information about dangerous areas that are provided by other users and its local Emergency hotlines.

3. Can it keep me updated?

Yes, you are being updated in real-time by your fellow user, who is concerned about the hazards in the area.

4. Do I have to be a Makinabang resident to use this website?

No, anyone interested can join. As long as you are willing to participate in keeping Barangay Makinabang safe especially during calamities.

5. Do we have to pay for anything?

Everything is free, and only participation is needed. This website is open to all and free to use


Purpose Of The Website

We aim to connect residents of Makinabang and outsiders through this informative and social platform in order for them to work together and achieve a safe and hazard-free environment. It will also give people a sense of unity because they are the ones who report the hazards and express their concerns. Last but not least, it helps keep people healthy and safe from injury or illness caused by flooding or other disasters.
